🎯 High-Quality Audience Composition

The subscriber base includes 42% decision-makers (VP-level and above), representing 193 senior professionals. This high concentration of leadership positions demonstrates strong appeal among influential thought leaders and decision-makers in the 2nd Order Thinkers community.
